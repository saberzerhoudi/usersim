Our Mission

To create a collaborative platform that brings together researchers, practitioners, and students interested in user simulation techniques. We aim to advance the field by sharing knowledge, tools, and best practices for evaluating information access systems through computational user modeling.

Our Vision

We envision a future where user simulation becomes a standard evaluation methodology across search engines, recommender systems, and conversational AI. By fostering an open community, we strive to accelerate research progress and bridge the gap between academic innovation and real-world applications.
