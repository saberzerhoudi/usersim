Building a large community for user simulation research

This community brings together researchers and practitioners working on user simulation for information access systems. Our mission is to advance the field through collaboration, sharing open-source tools, research publications, and organizing workshops and tutorials. We welcome contributions from diverse backgrounds including information retrieval, dialogue systems, machine learning, user modeling, and economics.
