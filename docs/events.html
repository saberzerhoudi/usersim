Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about user simulation events, workshops, and community activities.

What types of events are organized?
We organize workshops and tutorials at leading conferences in information retrieval, artificial intelligence, and web technologies. Our events cover topics including user simulation techniques, system evaluation methods, and synthetic data generation for information access systems.
How can I participate in upcoming events?
Upcoming workshops and tutorials are typically announced on conference websites and our community channels. Registration is usually handled through the main conference registration system. Check the event descriptions for specific submission deadlines and participation requirements.
Are event materials and recordings available?
Materials from past events, including slides, tutorials, and workshop reports, are often made available through conference proceedings or our community resources. Some events marked with 'Report' links have detailed summaries available. Check individual event descriptions for access to specific materials.
How can I stay updated on new events?
To stay informed about upcoming workshops, tutorials, and events, join our community mailing list or follow conference announcements. New events are typically announced several months in advance through conference websites and community channels. Check back regularly for updates on future events.
